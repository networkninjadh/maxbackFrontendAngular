  1. When can I expect my refund after I e-file my taxes?
    2. You will receive your federal refund within 3 weeks of filing your tax return. The IRS states that the refunds will be issued in less than 21 days.

  2. How long does it takes to process my tax return?
    3. Processing time for e-filed tax returns with direct deposit can expect to be processed within 21 days of IRS e-file acceptance date. Mailed paper returns processing time is 6 to 8 weeks from the date the IRS accepts the tax return.

  3. How can I find out if I owe any money for taxes or other debts?
    4. You can contact the BFS's TOP(offset #) call center at 800-304-3107 or TDD 866-297-0517, Monday through Friday 7:30 a.m to 5p.m CST. You can contact the IRS if your original tax refund amount on your BFS offset notice is different than the amount shown on your tax return.

  4. Can I speak with a tax preparer online about my taxes?
    5. You can speak to a tax preparer regarding your taxes once an account is created and an appointment is booked to video chat with a 1-on-1 interview with tax preparer to e-file tax return.

  5. How long does it take for my state taxes to be processed?
    6. For most states e-filed returns can expect a processing time of three weeks if you use direct deposit, however, paper returns takes about six weeks.
    Some states have different guidelines when a tax refund can be expected.
    California processing time is between seven and 12 business days for e-filed returns. Paper returns processing time is two to three months. Please vist California tax refund website to check refund status.
    New York processing time for refunds is within three weeks and paper returns takes about six weeks. Please vist New York tax refund website to check refund status.
    Illinois processing time for refunds is within two to three weeks for e-filed returns. Please vist Illinois tax refund website to check refund status.

  6. How many dependent exemptions can I carry?
    7. IRS states that a taxpayer can receive EIC for a maximum of 3 dependents. Depending on the income earned child related credits are affected.

  7. What documents will I need to provide to IRS if I get audited?
    8. Depending on the audit many documents could be requested such as; Receipts, Bills, Canceled checks, Legal papers, Divorce settlements, Property acquisition, Loan agreements, Logs or diaries, Tickets, Medical and Dental records. Please refer to IRS.gov for more information.

  8. How can I check the status of my refund?
    9. You can check the status of your refund on Where's My Refund website

  9. How much can I get back on my tax return?
    10. You can click on the services page and get a free estimate of your taxes based on the income earned and other information provided from your tax documents.

  10. What are my refund options?
    11. MaxBac allows a taxpayer to choose direct deposit, a paper check, a netspend visa card, or walmart to walmart for refund options.

  11. How long does it take to process an amended tax return?
    12. IRS states that a form 1040X processing time can take up to 16 weeks once IRS receives it. After mailing the return it will take up to 3 weeks to show up in IRS system.
